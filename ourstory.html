Our Story

Welcome to the Freedom Project, an Irish vanlife blog! My name is George, I'm 25, and I live and travel full-time in my van, 'Goats Cheese', with my little rotti companion, Zayda. Originally from Dublin, we travel all over Ireland and soon Europe, surfing, hiking, and exploring.

So why, vanlife? Three reasons, to travel, to experience more of the outdoors, and to live with less. Striping your life back to what you can fit in a 3m x 2m space is challenging but so rewarding. It has forced me to shift my perspective on what's important and what I fundamentally need to be happy.

So why the blog? With the size of the vanlife community worldwide, there is no shortage of resources available for people looking to take on their own conversions or to get a glimpse of what living and travelling full-time in a van is really like. When doing my own research, I found there to be far less information out there from an Irish perspective. Getting a van on the road in Ireland is surprisingly tricky. There are a lot of hoops to jump through when it comes to converting, registering, and insuring, so I decided to document my experience to help others who might be thinking of building a tiny home on wheels, for weekends away or for full-time living.

I hope you find the resources here helpful and if you have any questions, please feel free to get in touch either here or on social media.
