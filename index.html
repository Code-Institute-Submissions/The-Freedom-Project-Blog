Our Story

Welcome to the Freedom Project, an Irish vanlife blog! My name is George; I live and travel full-time in my van, 'Goats Cheese', with my little rotti companion, Zayda. Originally from Dublin, we travel all over Ireland and Europe, surfing, hiking, and exploring. On this blog, I have detailed my entire conversion experience, do's and don'ts, what I would change, what I would keep, how to get a van on the road in Ireland (registration, tax, and insurance), and an inside look into full-time vanlife in Ireland. You can read more about our story here. I hope you enjoy, and should you have any questions, don't be afraid to get in touch!
